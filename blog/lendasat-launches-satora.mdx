We built satora because moving between Bitcoin and everything else shouldn't require trust.

Today, if you want to swap BTC for stablecoins or other assets, you're forced to rely on centralized exchanges, custodial bridges, or wrapped tokens. Every one of these introduces a counterparty - someone who holds your funds and can freeze, delay, or lose them.

satora eliminates that entirely.

## What satora is

satora is a trustless, atomic swapping service. It lets users swap between Bitcoin (on-chain and Lightning) and assets on other chains - without intermediaries, without custody, without risk of losing funds to a third party.

Every swap is atomic: either both sides complete, or neither does. There's no window where someone holds your money. No bridging risk. No wrapped tokens.

## Built for integration

satora isn't just a product - it's infrastructure. We built it as an SDK so that wallets, exchanges, payment processors, and any application can integrate trustless swaps directly into their product.

Whether you're building a Lightning wallet that needs fiat offramps, a merchant tool that accepts Bitcoin, or a DeFi protocol that wants native BTC liquidity - satora gives you a single integration to make it work.

## The goal

The world of Bitcoin and the world of everything else shouldn't be separate. Moving value between them should be as seamless as sending a message - instant, private, and without needing to trust anyone along the way.

That's what we're building. That's satora.
