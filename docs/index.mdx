## Why Satora Swaps?

Satora Swaps is designed for developers and power users who need a seamless, non-custodial bridge between Bitcoin and stablecoins.

- **Non-Custodial**: You retain control through atomic swaps (HTLCs).
- **Fast**: Swaps complete in minutes via Lightning/Arkade/On-chain.
- **Gasless**: Satora covers gas fees on all EVM chains (Polygon, Ethereum, Arbitrum) for token claims.
- **Multi-Token**: Swap to supported ERC-20 tokens via DEX routing.
- **Developer First**: Idiomatic TypeScript SDK.
